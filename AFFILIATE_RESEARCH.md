- **Status:** CONFIRMED — verified from adobe.com/affiliates.html on 2026-03-16

- **Commission structure:**
  - Monthly subscriptions: 85% of the first month's subscription fee

- **Self-serve:** Yes (via Partnerize platform), but requires approval
- **Notes:** Solid commissions. Relevance is medium for TikTok creators — higher-end creators who also do YouTube or brand work. Adobe Express is more accessible for beginners.

## Category 4: Equipment (Lighting, Audio, Camera)

### Amazon Associates (CURRENT — already active)
- **Status:** CONFIRMED — verified from affiliate-program.amazon.com on 2026-03-16
- **Program name:** Amazon Associates
- **Sign-up URL:** https://affiliate-program.amazon.com/signup
- **Network:** Direct (Amazon)
- **Commission structure:** Up to 10% across categories. Key rates for equipment:
  - Electronics (cameras, mics): 3-4%
  - Wireless/accessories: 4%
  - Musical instruments (including audio gear): 4-5%
  - Cell phones: 4%
  - Most physical products: 3-8% depending on subcategory
- **Cookie:** 24 hours (critical limitation — purchase must occur within 24 hours of click)
- **Approval:** Self-serve application. Requires generating qualifying sales within 180 days or account closes.
- **Self-serve:** Yes
- **Notes:** Already active. Cookie is the major weakness — 24 hours means impulse-purchase intent content works best (listicles, "gear I use" style posts where user is ready to buy). Keep and expand for equipment guides, but supplement with B&H Photo for higher-consideration gear purchases.

- **Sign-up URL:** https://member.impactradius.com/campaign-campaign-info/Skillshare.brand
