## 3. Key Facts & Data Points

**Eligibility requirements (verified against TikTok Support + Creator Academy, March 2026):**
- Minimum age: 18 years old
- Follower threshold: at least 10,000 authentic followers
- View threshold: at least 100,000 authentic video views in the **last 30 days** (rolling window — this trips people up)
- Account type: Personal accounts only. Business accounts are ineligible. Political/government accounts are ineligible.
- Account standing: Must be in good standing (no outstanding Community Guidelines violations)
- Geography: Must be a legal resident of an eligible country — US, UK, Germany, Japan, South Korea, France, Mexico, Brazil (8 countries as of March 2026)
- No fraudulent activity or artificial views

**How to apply (step-by-step, verified via TikTok Support):**
1. Open TikTok app on mobile
2. Go to your Profile (bottom right)
3. Tap the three-line menu (hamburger icon, top right)
4. Tap "Creator tools"
5. Tap "Creator Rewards Program" (or "Creativity Program Beta" — label may vary by account)
6. Tap "Apply"
7. Review and accept the terms
8. Wait for approval — typically within 3 days

**Content requirements once approved:**
- Videos must be at least 1 minute long to earn rewards
- Content must be original, filmed/designed/produced entirely by the creator
- Must be uploaded after joining the program
- Videos must receive at least 1,000 qualified For You feed views to earn

**Common disqualifying mistakes:**
- Using a Business account (switch to Personal first — guide covers this)
- Applying before 30-day rolling view window hits 100K
- Country mismatch (account registered in ineligible country)
- Prior violations under active review

**Countries NOT eligible (notable gaps):**
- Canada — confirmed excluded, no official timeline for inclusion [UNVERIFIED: no TikTok primary source on Canada exclusion explicitly in their help docs — confirmed by community consensus only]
- Australia — not currently eligible, flagged as potential 2026 addition [UNVERIFIED]
