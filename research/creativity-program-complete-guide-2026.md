## 3. Key Facts & Data Points

**Program history (verified):**
- TikTok Creator Fund launched in 2020 — paid $0.02-$0.04 per 1,000 views
- Creator Rewards Program (CRP) launched in 2023 as replacement
- Old Creator Fund officially retired in US/UK/France/Germany by January 2024
- CRP pays 20-30x more than Creator Fund per view [community-reported, not TikTok official]

**Eligibility (confirmed):**
- 10,000 followers minimum
- 100,000 video views in last 30 days (rolling window)
- 18+ years old
- Personal account only
- Eligible countries: US, UK, Germany, Japan, South Korea, France, Mexico, Brazil

**How earnings are calculated:**
- Rewards = Qualified Views × RPM
- RPM = Revenue Per 1,000 qualified views
- Qualified views: unique views from the For You feed; excludes views under 5 seconds, paid views, disliked views, fraud/artificial views
- RPM range (community-reported): $0.40–$1.00+ per 1,000 qualified views [UNVERIFIED — TikTok does not publish official RPM figures]
- Additional Rewards: bonus layer on top of base RPM; can add 50–90% to earnings; criteria are partially opaque [UNVERIFIED — community-reported percentage range]

**Content requirements:**
- Videos must be 1+ minutes long
- Original content only (no reposts, no reaction videos without original layer)
- Uploaded after enrollment date
- Must reach at least 1,000 qualified FYP views per video to earn

**Payment facts (confirmed):**
- Paid on the 15th of each month
- Minimum payout threshold: $50.00 USD
- Payment methods: PayPal or direct bank transfer
- If earnings don't hit $50, they roll over to next month
- Processing time varies by payment method (PayPal: 3–7 business days)

**What affects RPM most (verified by TikTok's documented RPM factors):**
1. Watch time and completion rate
2. Search performance (SEO value of the video)
3. Geographic distribution of viewers (US/UK views earn more)
4. Follower engagement rate
5. Advertiser demand in the content niche

**What CRP does NOT do:**
- Does not pay per follower
- Does not guarantee earnings — some videos earn $0
- Does not apply to videos shorter than 1 minute
- Does not pay retroactively for videos uploaded before joining
