### Business Account Disqualification from Creator Rewards (Ongoing, flagged in creator community April 2026)
**[FACT]** Creators with business-type accounts are being disqualified from the Creator Rewards Program. Only personal accounts in good standing are eligible. Business accounts and accounts associated with political or government organizations are excluded by program terms. TikTok allows an appeal within 30 days; if successful, reinstatement is automatic. Recommended fix: switch to a personal account.
Source: [TikTok Support — Creator Rewards Program](https://support.tiktok.com/en/business-and-creator/creator-rewards-program/creator-rewards-program), [TikTok Creator Academy](https://www.tiktok.com/creator-academy/en/article/creator-rewards-program)

Source: Creator video [TikTok @calledandcapable](https://www.tiktok.com/@calledandcapable/video/7516699218954718494)

### Creator Health Rating (CHR) — Fully Replaced Violation Points (January 2026)
**[FACT]** TikTok replaced its Violation Points system with the Creator Health Rating (CHR) between January 12–19, 2026. CHR operates on a 0–1,000 scale, with creators starting at 200 points. Compliant behavior earns points; violations deduct them. The system is designed to reward good behavior as much as penalize violations.
Source: [Disrupt Marketing](https://disruptmarketing.co/blog/tiktok-updates/)
