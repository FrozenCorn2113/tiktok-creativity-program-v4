**Category breakdown:**

### Video Editing
- **CapCut** — free, ByteDance-owned, built-in TikTok trend library, auto-captions, templates. Privacy concerns flagged (ByteDance connection). No affiliate program confirmed.
- **Filmora** (Windows/Mac) — desktop-grade editing, robust timeline. PartnerStack affiliate, ~30-40% commission. Recommended for Windows/Mac creators who outgrow CapCut.
- **InShot** — mobile-first, strong for quick cuts and speed control. No affiliate program confirmed.
- **Adobe Premiere Rush** / **Adobe Creative Cloud** — professional tier; Partnerize affiliate, 85% first month commission. Recommended for creators scaling to full production.
- **CapCut Pro** — paid tier, clarity effects and AI tools [UNVERIFIED pricing — need to verify against tools page]
- **VEED.io** — browser-based, auto-captions, good for creators without mobile editing preference

### Analytics
- **TikTok Studio** (native) — free, built into app/desktop. Shows views, completion rate, follower analytics, RPM per video. Limitation: only 60 days historical data.
- **Pentos** — TikTok-specific, trend intelligence, hashtag performance, competitor tracking. Pricing confirmed from Phase 2 research: $99/month (Trends only), $299/month (1 account), $999/month (10 accounts). No affiliate program confirmed.
- **Exolyt** — profile + competitor benchmarking, viral trigger analysis, hashtag/sound tracking. No affiliate program confirmed.
- **Analisa.io** — TikTok + Instagram analytics, historical data, engagement analysis. No affiliate program confirmed.
- **Social Blade** — free tier, follower growth tracking, historical comparison. Free with paid tiers.

### Scheduling
- **TikTok's native scheduler** — free, available for Business + Creator accounts, schedules up to 10 days in advance
- **Later** — visual content calendar, optimal time suggestions, cross-platform. Impact affiliate, ~30% recurring commission, 90-day cookie. Best affiliate in this category.
- **Buffer** — simpler interface, affordable entry point, TikTok scheduling + basic analytics. Impact affiliate program [UNVERIFIED commission rate — check Impact for current terms]
- **SocialBee** — $29/month, auto-publish, AI caption tools, multi-account. Affiliate program [UNVERIFIED]
- **OpusClip** — AI-powered long-to-short video repurposing + built-in TikTok scheduling calendar

### Audio / Music
- **Epidemic Sound** — licensed music for TikTok (CRP-safe). Impact affiliate, ~50% first payment.
- **Artlist** — licensed tracks and SFX. CJ Affiliate program (Cloudflare-blocked during research; exists per artlist.io source). Best for creators who need film-quality audio.
- TikTok's commercial sound library (free, built-in) — sufficient for most CRP creators

### Link-in-Bio / Monetization
- **Stan** — link-in-bio + digital product sales, creator-native
- **Beacons** — free link-in-bio with analytics
- **Kit (ConvertKit)** — email list building. PartnerStack, 50% commission for 12 months. Best affiliate value in this guide.
