## 3. Key Facts & Data Points

**Why 1,000 followers matters for CRP path:**
- 1,000 followers: unlocks TikTok Live
- 10,000 followers: minimum for Creator Rewards Program eligibility
- The journey from 0 to 1K is the hardest barrier — once algorithm "learns" account, growth accelerates

**Algorithm fundamentals (2026, verified from multiple industry sources):**
- Completion rate and rewatch rate are the #1 ranking signal (~40-50% of algorithm weight)
- 2026 update: "follower-first testing" — new videos are shown to existing followers first; engagement from followers determines broader distribution [reported by multiple industry analyses, not official TikTok doc]
- Shares and saves now outweigh likes in the algorithm [industry consensus, not official]
- Comment quality (length and depth) matters more than comment count [UNVERIFIED]
- Virality completion rate threshold raised to ~70% in 2026, up from ~50% in 2024 [UNVERIFIED — multiple industry sources]

**Profile optimization facts:**
- Profile picture, bio, and pinned video all affect follow conversion rate
- TikTok search is used by 49% of US consumers (Adobe study, January 2026) — bio keywords affect search discoverability
- Gen Z: 64% use TikTok for search; 51% prefer it over Google for information discovery (Adobe, Jan 2026)

**Growth tactics (community-verified, cross-referenced):**
- Niche specificity: hyper-specific niches outperform general "lifestyle" accounts significantly at early stages
- Educational content drives Saves most effectively — saves signal high value to algorithm
- Hook optimization: 71% of whether users continue watching is determined in the first 3 seconds [multiple industry sources]
- 3+3 hashtag formula: 3 broad trending hashtags + 3 niche-specific hashtags
- Respond to comments within first hour of posting — creates engagement spike
- Optimal video length for new accounts: 15-30 seconds (higher completion rate when short)
- Post frequency: consistent daily posting recommended for new accounts
- Posting time: evenings (5-10pm ET) Tue-Thu and Saturday midday show strongest engagement [from Phase 2 research on best-posting-schedule]

**Timeline expectations:**
- Purely organic, consistent quality content: 2-4 weeks to 1K [UNVERIFIED — community average]
- Creators combining strategic content + engagement tactics: 3-7 days to 1K [UNVERIFIED — frequently cited, varies widely]
- Reality check: timelines vary enormously by niche, content quality, and consistency
