## 1. Current Program Details

### Market Context
Ring lights are the #1 lighting purchase for face-to-camera TikTok creators. The market is dominated by budget Amazon brands (UBeesize, Sensyne, Weilisi, STALLY, Kaiess) at the entry level and professional brands (Neewer, Rotolight, Westcott) at the premium tier. Most TikTok creators land in the $20–$120 range.

**The dominant use case for TikTok:** Ring lights are specifically suited to talking-head, beauty, and lifestyle content where the creator is stationary in frame. They produce the characteristic "catchlight" reflection in eyes that signals "this is a creator video." They are not suited for wide shots or moving content.

**Key specification variables creators encounter:**
- **Size:** 10–22 inches. 10–12" is desktop/phone setup; 18–22" is larger floor stand setups used for beauty/makeup
- **Color temperature:** Typically 3000K (warm) to 6000K (cool/daylight). Adjustable temperature is a key differentiator
- **Dimmability:** Most modern ring lights offer 10+ brightness levels
- **Mount type:** Phone holder vs tripod vs desk clamp — critical for TikTok mobile workflows
- **Power:** USB-powered vs AC vs battery. Battery/wireless is premium
- **CRI (Color Rendering Index):** Higher = more accurate colors. Matters for beauty content where color fidelity is commercial-critical

**The two distinct market segments:**
1. **Phone/mobile creators (majority of TikTok users):** Need phone holder mount, compact size, USB power, affordable. Budget $20–$60.
2. **Camera creators (serious TikTok creators, transition to YouTube/brand work):** Need larger light, higher CRI, camera mount compatibility. Budget $80–$200+.

### TikTok-Specific Evaluation Criteria (Scribe should use these as the rubric)
1. **Phone holder quality and angle range** — can it hold a modern iPhone/Android securely at portrait angle?
2. **Height range** — does the tripod reach standing height for creators who film standing up?
3. **Color temperature range** — warm to cool; adjustable is significantly better for different skin tones and room lighting
4. **Dimmability** — 10+ levels minimum; stepless dimming is premium
5. **Power source** — USB vs AC vs battery; battery enables outdoor/location use
6. **Setup time** — TikTok creators shoot spontaneously; a ring light that takes 10+ minutes to set up won't get used
7. **Portability** — fits in a bag? Matters for mobile creators
8. **Portrait vs landscape** — TikTok is portrait; some ring light-phone setups are awkward for full 9:16 framing
