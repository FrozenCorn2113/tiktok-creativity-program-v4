## 3. Key Facts & Data Points

**Why watch time is the #1 metric for CRP earnings (verified):**
- Qualified views exclude views under 5 seconds — you must hold attention past 5 seconds to earn anything
- RPM is directly influenced by watch time: higher completion rate = higher RPM [TikTok official RPM factors documentation]
- Completion rate is the single strongest algorithm signal for wider distribution [multiple industry sources, consistent with TikTok Creator Academy guidance]
- 2026 virality threshold: ~70% completion rate (up from ~50% in 2024) [UNVERIFIED — industry consensus]

**Hook data (cross-referenced from Phase 2 research and 2026 sources):**
- 71% of whether users continue watching is determined in the first 3 seconds [industry sources, not TikTok official]
- Videos with 3-second retention above 65% receive 4-7x more impressions [UNVERIFIED — OpusClip data]
- Visual hook must work on mute (many viewers start with sound off)
- Proven hook types: curiosity gap ("Why did 10,000 people unfollow me?"), pattern interruption, social proof, incomplete information

**Video length and completion rate:**
- Minimum for CRP earnings: 1 minute
- Sweet spot for retention + monetization: 60-90 seconds [multiple industry analyses]
- For CRP purposes, longer is better IF you can hold attention — a 90-second video with 75% completion earns more than a 60-second video with 40% completion
- Algorithm prioritizes: total watch time × completion rate (not just one metric alone)
- A video keeping 80% of viewers for 20 seconds outperforms one keeping 50% for 40 seconds in algorithmic terms [OpusClip, attribution noted]

**Retention structure techniques (verified community + industry):**
- Loop structure: end video with same hook/question as beginning — creates rewatch
- Pattern interrupts every 8-12 seconds to reset attention
- "B-roll switching" — cut to different visual every few seconds even if audio is continuous
- Cliffhanger structure: don't answer the question until the last 15 seconds
- Open loops: tease 3 things at start, deliver all 3 — viewers stay through the list

**CRP-specific watch time connection:**
- TikTok's documented RPM factors include: watch time/completion, search performance, geographic distribution, follower engagement, advertiser demand
- The creator who documented 8% → 31% rewatch rate improvement went from 255 avg views to 18K in 4 weeks (anonymous source from r/TikTokMonetizing thread /1r9rapl/) [UNVERIFIED — community thread, no named creator]
- Track RPM per video in TikTok Studio — reverse-engineer top earners by length, format, and topic

**Tools for watch time analysis:**
- TikTok Studio native analytics: shows average watch time, completion rate per video (free)
- Pentos: trend tracking, historical data beyond 60 days (paid — $99/month Trends only, $299/month 1 account)
- Exolyt: competitor benchmarking, engagement analytics
