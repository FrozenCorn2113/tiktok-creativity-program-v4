## 1. TikTok Platform & Policy Developments

### TikTok / Cameo Partnership (NEW — March 31, 2026)
**[FACT]** TikTok announced a partnership with Cameo on March 31, 2026, live in early April. U.S. creators can now add a call-to-action button to their TikTok posts allowing fans to request personalized Cameo videos directly within the TikTok app. Creators link their existing Cameo account or onboard new via the partnership. **[FACT]** Only U.S. creators confirmed in initial rollout. No international availability announced.

**[FACT]** CEO Steven Galanis stated TikTok creators had Cameo's "strongest year yet" in 2025 on the platform, making this a formalizing of an already active creator segment.

### TikTok Creator Rewards Eligibility — No Change Confirmed
**[FACT]** As of this scan, the TikTok Creator Rewards Program eligibility requirements remain unchanged: 10,000 followers, 100,000 views in the last 30 days, age 18+, personal account, eligible country, account in good standing.
Source: [TikTok Support](https://support.tiktok.com/en/business-and-creator/creator-rewards-program/creator-rewards-program), [TikTok Creator Academy](https://www.tiktok.com/creator-academy/article/creator-rewards-program/?lang=en)

This claim is contradicted by TikTok's official support page and Creator Academy article, which still list 10,000/100,000 as of this scan. Do NOT propagate this claim.

### Meta Creator Fast Track (Competitor Development — March 18, 2026)
**[FACT]** Meta launched "Creator Fast Track" on Facebook on March 18, 2026. The program pays established creators guaranteed monthly minimums for posting 15 Reels/month on Facebook:
- $1,000/month for creators with 100,000+ followers on Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube
- $3,000/month for creators with 1,000,000+ followers on at least one of those platforms

Payments are guaranteed for three months. After the Fast Track period, creators retain access to Facebook Content Monetization for ongoing earnings.
Source: [Meta Newsroom](https://about.fb.com/news/2026/03/creator-fast-track-grow-your-audience-earn-money-on-facebook/), [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/18/facebook-launches-a-new-monetization-program-to-attract-popular-creators-from-tiktok-youtube/), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/18/meta-creator-pay-instagram-tiktok-youtube-facebook.html)
