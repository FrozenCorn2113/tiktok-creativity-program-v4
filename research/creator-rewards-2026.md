### RPM Ranges (critical data — verify against current creator reports)
RPM = Revenue Per Mille (per 1,000 qualified views). This is the fundamental metric.

**Widely reported ranges as of late 2025/early 2026:**
- Overall average: $0.40–$1.00 per 1,000 qualified views [UNVERIFIED — community-reported range; highly variable by niche, content type, geography]
- High-performing niches (finance, business, tech): $0.80–$2.00+ per 1,000 qualified views [UNVERIFIED]
- Low-performing niches (entertainment, dance, general): $0.20–$0.60 per 1,000 qualified views [UNVERIFIED]
- US-based creators typically earn 2–4x more than non-US creators for equivalent views [UNVERIFIED — widely cited but hard to source precisely]

**What "qualified views" means:**
A qualified view is NOT the same as a view. TikTok applies a quality filter. Factors that reduce qualification rate:
- Short watch time (viewer swipes away before 50% completion — estimated)
- Suspected bot/low-quality traffic
- Views from ineligible regions
- Replay views after a certain threshold (estimated)
- Views from the creator themselves

TikTok does not publicly define the exact qualified view algorithm. This opacity is a core creator frustration and directly drives search traffic to troubleshooting guides.
