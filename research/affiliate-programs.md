## Tier 1: Apply NOW (Easy Approval)

### Amazon Associates
- **Covers:** All physical gear — ring lights (Neewer, UBeesize), microphones (BOYA, RODE, Sennheiser, Mackie, Maono)
- **Signup URL:** https://affiliate-program.amazon.com/
- **Commission:** 1-4% depending on category
- **Cookie:** 24 hours (90 days if added to cart)
- **Approval:** Easy — need 3 sales in first 180 days, site needs 10+ posts
- **Recommendation:** APPLY NOW — covers all physical products in one shot
- **Notes:** Must add disclosure: "As an Amazon Associate, I earn from qualifying purchases"

### Kit (ConvertKit)
- **Signup URL:** https://kit.com/affiliate
- **Commission:** 50% for first 12 months, then 10-20% recurring (tiered by referrals)
- **Cookie:** 60 days
- **Approval:** Easy — free to join, no account required
- **Recommendation:** APPLY NOW — high commission, we already recommend Kit heavily

### Buffer
- **Signup URL:** https://buffer.com/partners
- **Commission:** 25% monthly for 12 months
- **Cookie:** 30 days
- **Approval:** Easy — open to creators, bloggers, newsletters
- **Recommendation:** APPLY NOW

### Later
- **Signup URL:** https://later.com/affiliate-program/

- **Cookie:** 30 days
- **Approval:** Easy — free to join
- **Recommendation:** APPLY NOW — we already reference Later in multiple guides

### Kajabi

### Gumroad
