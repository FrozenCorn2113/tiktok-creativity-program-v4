## 1. Current Program Details

### Market Context
TikTok analytics tools serve two distinct audiences with very different needs:

1. **Individual creators** — want to understand their own content performance: which videos drove followers, what's their best posting time, is their RPM improving, which hooks get the most watch time
2. **Brands and agencies** — want competitor analysis, influencer discovery, campaign tracking, trend monitoring

This creates a significant content problem: most "best TikTok analytics tools" guides are written for the brand/agency buyer (expensive enterprise tools) but the query is primarily asked by individual creators. This is the guide's primary competitive angle.

**TikTok's native analytics** (Creator Center) is free, increasingly capable, and often sufficient for solo creators. The case for third-party tools needs to be honest: native analytics has real limitations, but most $100+/month tools are for agencies, not individual creators.

## 5. Category-Specific Research (Analytics Tools)

### Tool-by-Tool Deep Dive

**TikTok Creator Center / Native Analytics (Free — No Affiliate)**
- Access: creators.tiktok.com or in-app Creator Tools
- Key dashboard sections:
  - Overview (followers, video views, profile views, likes)
  - Content (per-video performance: views, average watch time, full video views, traffic sources)
  - Followers (demographics: top territories, gender, age, active times)
  - LIVE data (if applicable)
- What native analytics does NOT show:
  - Competitor data
  - Historical data beyond 60 days
  - CRP-specific qualified view data (that's in Creator Center Rewards section, separate from analytics)
  - Engagement rate benchmarks vs similar accounts
- Recommendation: Use this before considering any paid tool
