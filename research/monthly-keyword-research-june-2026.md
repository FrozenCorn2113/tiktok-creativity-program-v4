**Priority:** MEDIUM-HIGH (new gap, feature is live and growing)
**Recommended slug:** `tiktok-bulletin-boards-creators`

**Difficulty:** Low — feature is live but educational content is shallow across the web

**Why it qualifies:**
- [FACT] TikTok officially launched Bulletin Boards on January 31, 2026. The feature is available to creators with 50,000+ followers who are 18+. It is TikTok's equivalent of Instagram's Broadcast Channels — a one-to-many direct communication channel to subscribers. Source: [TikTok Newsroom — Introducing Bulletin Board](https://newsroom.tiktok.com/introducing-bulletin-board?lang=en), [Social Media Today](https://www.socialmediatoday.com/news/tiktok-launches-bulletin-board-broadcast-messaging-option/805466/)
- [FACT] Bulletin Boards support text, image, and video posts. Creators can post up to 20 bulletins per day. Followers opt in via the creator's profile page. Source: [TikTok Creator Academy — Bulletin Board](https://www.tiktok.com/creator-academy/article/bulletin-board)
- [INFERRED] Bulletin Boards have a documented indirect monetization value: they convert passive followers into opted-in subscribers, support Shop promotion, enable LIVE announcements, and strengthen the direct creator-fan relationship that drives gift income and brand deal value.
- No TCP guide covers this feature. The 50K threshold makes it highly relevant to TCP's audience — many readers are working toward or have recently passed this milestone.
- Competitor content on Bulletin Boards is mostly news-announcement format. A TCP-style creator strategy guide (how to use it, how to grow it, how it connects to monetization) would stand out.

**Key angles to cover:**
1. What Bulletin Boards are, eligibility (50K+, 18+), and how they differ from DMs and comments
2. How to set one up and what types of posts work (announcements, polls, exclusive content teasers)
3. How Bulletin Boards connect to monetization: LIVE announcements, Shop product drops, brand deal amplification
4. Growth strategies: why getting followers to join your board matters and how to invite them without being spammy
5. Bulletin Boards vs. TikTok Series vs. TikTok LIVE subscriptions — how they fit together

**Sources confirmed:** [TikTok Newsroom](https://newsroom.tiktok.com/introducing-bulletin-board?lang=en), [Social Media Today](https://www.socialmediatoday.com/news/tiktok-launches-bulletin-board-broadcast-messaging-option/805466/), [Business Tech Weekly](https://www.businesstechweekly.com/technology-news/tiktoks-new-bulletin-boards-a-game-changer-for-creators-with-50k-followers/), [TikTok Creator Academy](https://www.tiktok.com/creator-academy/article/bulletin-board)
