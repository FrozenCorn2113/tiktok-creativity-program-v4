## 1. Current Program Details

### RPM Definition and Calculation
RPM (Revenue Per Mille) in the Creator Rewards Program = earnings per 1,000 QUALIFIED views. This is fundamentally different from ad RPM on YouTube or a blog, where RPM is based on ad impressions.

**The RPM formula TikTok uses is not published.** What is known from community observation and inferred from TikTok's public statements:
- RPM is not fixed — it varies by video, by audience, by niche, by season, and by content performance signals

- A "Search Multiplier" was introduced (reportedly 2024) — content discovered via search earns a higher rate than content discovered via For You Page [UNVERIFIED — widely reported, no official announcement found]

## 5. Category-Specific Research (Strategy)

### The 6 Highest-Impact RPM Optimization Tactics

**Tactic 1: Post videos at least 2 minutes long (not just 1 minute)**
The 1-minute minimum is the floor for qualification. Community consensus suggests RPM increases significantly for videos in the 2–4 minute range. Mechanism: longer videos attract completion-oriented viewers, signal higher content value to the algorithm, and allow more ad opportunity. [UNVERIFIED — needs specific data point; the 2-minute vs 1-minute RPM difference is widely discussed but not officially confirmed]

**Tactic 2: Optimize for TikTok Search**
The "Search RPM" bonus is reportedly 2–5x the base FYP RPM. Optimization tactics:
- Include target keywords in the video title (which functions as a caption)
- Add relevant keywords to the on-screen text within the first 5 seconds
- Use descriptive, search-oriented captions (not just emoji chains)
- The TikTok search bar autocomplete shows real search terms — use it for keyword research
This is a fundamentally different creative approach from FYP optimization. [UNVERIFIED — needs official source or strong community documentation]

**Tactic 3: Grow a US-dominant audience**
The single highest-impact RPM lever. US viewers drive qualified views at dramatically higher rates than international viewers. Tactics for US audience growth:
- Post during US peak hours (7–9am ET, 6–8pm ET)
- Reference US-specific cultural touchpoints (US sports, holidays, news)
- Engage with US-based accounts in your niche
[These are community strategies — actual RPM impact [UNVERIFIED]]

**Tactic 4: Improve video completion rate**
Higher completion rate → more qualified views → more earnings. The mechanism is circular: a video that holds attention earns more per view AND gets distributed to more people by the algorithm.
- Pattern interrupt every 15–20 seconds to reset attention
- End strong (not fading out — viewers who drop at 90% still may not complete)
- Captions keep sound-off viewers engaged longer

**Tactic 5: Choose a high-RPM niche**
If content flexibility exists, finance/business/education niches consistently earn more per qualified view. Not always possible to switch niches, but a fitness creator who adds "business of fitness" content may see RPM lift.

**Tactic 6: Avoid "viral internationally" trap**
International virality looks great in analytics but earns very little. A video that's trending in India or Brazil may have 10M views and $30 in earnings. Creators should calibrate expectations and focus on US/UK audience retention rather than global viral reach.
