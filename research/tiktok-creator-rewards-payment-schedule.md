## 3. Key Facts & Data Points

**Payment date (confirmed via TikTok official documentation):**
- Rewards become redeemable on the **15th of each month**
- Covers the previous month's earnings
- The 15th is the target date; actual receipt depends on payment processor
- PayPal: typically 3–7 business days after the 15th
- Direct bank transfer: typically 3–5 business days
- Slight discrepancy in sources: some cite "15th to 20th" window; official terms say 15th [confirmed — the 15th is TikTok's release date, not guaranteed delivery date]

**Minimum payout threshold (confirmed):**
- US: $50.00 USD minimum
- EEA (UK, Germany, France): $50.00 USD equivalent in local currency
- If earnings don't reach $50 in a month, they roll over to the following month
- There is no time limit on rollover — balance accumulates until threshold is met

**Payment methods (confirmed):**
- PayPal
- Direct bank transfer (US creators — linked to Hyperwallet in some regions)
- Local payment processors in some countries (varies by region)
- Cryptocurrency is NOT a payment option

**How to set up payment (process):**
1. In TikTok app: go to Creator Tools
2. Tap on your Creator Rewards Program
3. Tap "Balance" or "Withdraw"
4. Add PayPal email or bank account details
5. Confirm identity if prompted (W-9 for US creators, W-8BEN for non-US)

**Tax requirements:**
- US creators: W-9 form required; TikTok issues 1099 at year end if earnings exceed $600
- Non-US creators: W-8BEN required (relevant for Canadian creators earning via other programs)
- TikTok Studio shows estimated earnings — final amounts may differ slightly after TikTok's monthly confirmation process

**Common payment delays and causes:**
- Payment not released: earnings haven't been confirmed by TikTok (confirmation happens before the 15th)
- Payment method not verified: PayPal email not confirmed, bank account not validated
- Account under review: any active violations pause payments
- Threshold not met: earnings below $50 roll over
- TikTok's review window: rewards are "estimated" during the month and "confirmed" just before payout — confirmed amount can differ from estimated

**Additional Rewards payment note:**
- Additional Rewards are included in the same monthly payment
- They appear as a separate line item in TikTok Studio earnings breakdown
- Additional Rewards can be unpredictable — confirmed on a monthly basis, not in real-time

**Payment schedule calendar (TikTok Studio feature):**
- TikTok Studio has a "payout schedule calendar" showing next payment dates
- Creator can track pending balance vs. confirmed balance in the Balance section

**Rollover mechanics:**
- If you earn $30 in January and $25 in February, you'll receive $55 in March (assuming no other deductions)
- New creators frequently hit a January/February trough if they joined mid-month
