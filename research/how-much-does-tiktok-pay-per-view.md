## 3. Key Facts & Data Points

**IMPORTANT: TikTok does not publish official RPM rates. All RPM figures are community-reported or derived from creator disclosures.**

**Creator Rewards Program (CRP) RPM — current best data (as of March 2026):**
- Community-reported range: $0.40 to $1.00 per 1,000 qualified views [UNVERIFIED — multiple industry sources, cross-referenced]
- Some creators report up to $2.00+ per 1,000 views in high-RPM niches [UNVERIFIED]
- Average reported payout: approximately $0.80 per 1,000 views (equivalent to ~$807 per million views) [UNVERIFIED — DemandSage estimate]
- Earnings for 1 million views: $400–$1,000 range [UNVERIFIED — industry consensus across multiple sources]

**Old Creator Fund (retired 2024) for contrast:**
- Paid $0.02–$0.04 per 1,000 views
- CRP improvement: approximately 20-30x more per view [community-reported]

**What "per view" actually means (important nuance):**
- TikTok pays per QUALIFIED view, not every view
- Qualified view = unique For You feed view; watched more than 5 seconds; not paid/promoted/artificial
- A video with 1 million total views may only have 700K qualified views that count toward CRP earnings
- This is why two creators can report very different earnings from "the same number of views"

**Factors that change your per-view rate (all five are TikTok-documented RPM factors):**
1. **Watch time and completion rate** — higher completion = higher RPM
2. **Search performance** — videos answering search queries earn higher RPM
3. **Geographic distribution** — US/UK/German views earn 2-5x more than Tier 2 country views [UNVERIFIED multiplier range, directionally confirmed]
4. **Follower engagement rate** — engaged follower base boosts RPM
5. **Advertiser demand in your niche** — finance/business/education content commands premium CPM

**Niche RPM differences (community data, all UNVERIFIED):**
- Finance/investing: $1.00–$2.00+ per 1,000 views
- Education/how-to: $0.80–$1.50 per 1,000 views
- Fitness/wellness: $0.60–$1.20 per 1,000 views
- Beauty/fashion: $0.60–$1.80 per 1,000 views
- Comedy/entertainment: $0.02–$0.35 per 1,000 views (teen/global audience)
- Gaming: $0.10–$0.40 per 1,000 views (depending on content type)
- These are from Phase 2 research — consistent with community data, not official

**Geographic RPM impact:**
- Tier 1 audience (US, UK, Germany, Australia) = highest RPM
- Tier 2 (Brazil, Southeast Asia, India) = dramatically lower CPM for advertisers = lower RPM for creators
- A video that goes viral globally can have lower RPM than a video that stays in the US/UK ecosystem

**Additional Rewards layer:**
- CRP includes base RPM + "Additional Rewards" (bonus)
- Additional Rewards can represent 50-90% of total earnings in some cases [UNVERIFIED — community range]
- Opaque criteria — TikTok does not fully document what triggers Additional Rewards
- Explains why two videos with identical qualified view counts can show very different total earnings

**Benchmark examples (for Scribe to use with appropriate [UNVERIFIED] tags):**
- 100,000 qualified views at $0.60 RPM = $60
- 500,000 qualified views at $0.80 RPM = $400
- 1,000,000 qualified views at $0.80 RPM = $800
- These are directionally accurate but individual results vary significantly
