# TikTok Creator Rewards Program: Eligibility Checklist

**Check every box before you apply. Miss one and you get rejected.**

---

## The 5 Requirements

### 1. Age: 18+ (19+ in South Korea)

- [ ] I am at least 18 years old

**How to verify:** TikTok uses the birthdate you entered when you created your account. You may be asked to confirm during the application.

**If you're under 18:** No workaround exists. Wait until you qualify.

---

### 2. Followers: 10,000 minimum

- [ ] I have at least 10,000 followers right now

**How to verify:** Profile page shows your follower count. TikTok checks this at the moment you submit, not when you start the application.

**If you're under 10K:** Focus on organic growth. Promoted followers count toward the total, but don't rely on paid boosts alone. See our guide on growing to 10K: tiktokcreativityprogram.com/guides/get-10000-followers-tiktok-2026

---

### 3. Views: 100,000 in the last 30 days

- [ ] I have 100,000+ video views in the rolling 30-day window

**How to verify:** Go to **Creator tools > Analytics > Content**. Look at total views for the past 30 days. Not lifetime views. The 30-day window rolls forward daily.

**If you're under 100K:** Keep posting consistently. The window moves, so a few strong videos can push you over. Applying at 150K-200K gives you a buffer in case TikTok's calculation window doesn't align perfectly with your analytics.

**Watch out:** Views from TikTok Promote (paid boosts) do NOT count toward this threshold.

---

### 4. Account Type: Personal account only

- [ ] My account is set to Personal (not Business)

**How to verify:** Go to **Settings and Privacy > Account > Account type**. If it says "Business Account," you need to switch.

**If you have a Business account:**
1. Go to Settings and Privacy > Account > Switch to personal account
2. Complete the switch
3. Wait 30 days (this wait is firm, no shortcut)
4. Then apply

This is the most common "silent" rejection. Your followers and views can look perfect, but a Business account gets you auto-rejected without a clear error message.

## Before You Hit Submit: Content Check

Even if you pass all 5 requirements, TikTok reviews your recent content. These will get you rejected:

| Red Flag | Fix It |
|---|---|
| Business account still active | Switch to personal, wait 30 days |
| Mostly Duets and Stitches | These don't qualify. Post 5-10 original 1+ minute videos first |
| Videos under 60 seconds | Only videos 1+ minute earn through Creator Rewards. Build a library of longer content |
| Repurposed content with watermarks | Remove watermarked content. Post originals |
| Contact info in your bio | Remove personal/business contact details before applying |
| Clickbait content in recent library | Clean up misleading titles and thumbnails |
| Community Guidelines violations | Address outstanding strikes. Appeal old ones if applicable |
| Low-quality formats (slideshows, split screens) | TikTok has flagged these as ineligible. Replace with original video content |

---

## How to Apply (Quick Steps)

1. Open TikTok > **Profile > Menu > Creator tools**
2. Select **Creator Rewards Program**
3. Review the in-app eligibility checklist
4. Submit your application
5. Wait 1-3 business days

After approval, only NEW videos earn. Older videos are permanently excluded. Post an original 1+ minute video on the day you're approved.

---

## Got Rejected?

**Video appeal (video disqualified after you join):** You have 80 days. Go to Creator Rewards dashboard, find the video, tap Appeal.

**Account appeal (application rejected):** You have 30 days from the rejection notification. Tap Appeal inside the notification.

Both types are reviewed in about 3 business days. If your rejection is for missing metrics (not enough followers, wrong account type), appealing won't help. Fix the issue and reapply.
