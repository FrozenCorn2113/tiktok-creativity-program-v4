## The 4 Factors That Set Your RPM

**1. Watch Time + Retention (heaviest weight)**
Videos where viewers stay past 60 seconds see meaningfully higher RPM. Completion rates above 80% separate top earners from average. More watch time = more ad inventory = higher payout.

**2. Originality**
Original audio, footage, and perspective score highest. Duets, Stitches, and repurposed content with watermarks earn zero or near-zero RPM. "Here's what happened when I tested this" beats "here's what the internet says" every time.

**3. Search Value**
Videos that answer specific search queries earn 2-5x the RPM of FYP-only content [ESTIMATED]. Search viewers have buying intent. Advertisers pay more to reach them.

**4. Engagement Quality**
Saves and shares count more than likes. Comments with real back-and-forth add to the signal. Strong engagement in the first 48 hours can unlock the Additional Reward bonus, which is what separates $1.00 RPM from $3.00+.

## Quick Tips to Push Your RPM Higher

**Pick your battles.** Your niche sets the ceiling. If you have flexibility in what you cover, tutorial and how-to framing outperforms opinion or reaction content by 2-3x in most niches.

**Target US/UK viewers.** Audience geography matters more than your own location. Creators with 80%+ US viewership consistently hit $1.00+ RPM. A viral video in non-eligible countries generates views that don't even qualify.

**Title for search, not for clever.** "How to pay less in taxes as a creator" outperforms "This changed everything" on search RPM. Match real queries.

**Post your best content in Q4.** October through December delivers 30-50% higher RPMs across all niches. January drops 40-60%. Time your strongest videos accordingly.

**Fix retention before anything else.** Watch time affects both your qualified view rate AND your RPM simultaneously. It is the single highest-leverage improvement for most creators.
