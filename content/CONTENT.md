## what-is-body

TikTok pays eligible creators based on qualified views — not follower count, not likes, not how many people saved your video. The Creator Rewards Program (formerly the Creativity Program, formerly the Creator Fund) replaced the old fund in 2023 and pays significantly more per view. But the eligibility requirements are strict, the qualified view threshold is specific, and the payout math is nothing like what most guides describe.

This site exists because finding accurate, current information about the program is hard. TikTok's help pages are vague. Most creator blogs cite numbers from 2021. We verify against the actual program rules and update when things change.
