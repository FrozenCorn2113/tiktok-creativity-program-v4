# Q4 TikTok Strategy: Capitalize on Holiday Content

Q4 is the highest-earning quarter for most creators. This guide shows how to plan content, offers, and brand deals.

<CalloutBox type="info" title="Quick facts">
  <ul>
    <li>Holiday content spikes CPM and RPM.</li>
    <li>Brands allocate bigger budgets in Q4.</li>
    <li>Plan 4–6 weeks ahead for best results.</li>
  </ul>
</CalloutBox>

## Q4 playbook

1. Plan holiday series content  
2. Pitch brands with Q4 stats  
3. Promote seasonal products or offers  
4. Track RPM weekly
