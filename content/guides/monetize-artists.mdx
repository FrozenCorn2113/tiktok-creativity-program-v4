# How Artists Make Money on TikTok

Artists can build strong income on TikTok without chasing constant virality. The key is pairing consistent content with clear offers. This guide shows the most reliable paths.

<CalloutBox type="info" title="Quick facts">
  <ul>
    <li>Process videos drive saves and long watch time.</li>
    <li>Prints and commissions outperform random merch.</li>
    <li>1+ minute content improves Creator Rewards eligibility.</li>
  </ul>
</CalloutBox>

## Revenue stream 1: Creator Rewards

Longer videos of your process or tutorials perform well and can qualify for RPM.

## Revenue stream 2: Prints and product drops

Limited drops create urgency and keep production manageable. Start with one best-selling design.

## Revenue stream 3: Commissions

Open 3–5 slots per month and showcase past work to increase demand.

## Revenue stream 4: Digital products

Sell brushes, templates, or a tutorial pack. These scale well without shipping.
