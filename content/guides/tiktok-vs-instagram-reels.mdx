# TikTok vs Instagram Reels: Which Pays Creators More?

Both platforms offer monetization, but they reward different content behaviors. This guide compares eligibility, payouts, and who should focus where.

<CalloutBox type="info" title="Quick facts">
  <ul>
    <li>TikTok rewards qualified 1+ minute views; Reels rewards ad revenue and bonuses.</li>
    <li>Reels has broader geographic availability.</li>
    <li>TikTok RPM can be stronger for niche expertise.</li>
  </ul>
</CalloutBox>

## Eligibility differences

TikTok Creator Rewards:
- 10K followers  
- 100K views in 30 days  
- Personal account  
- 1+ minute content  

Instagram Reels:
- Bonuses and ad revenue (varies by region)  
- Creator eligibility often depends on account standing  

## Payout model comparison

TikTok pays RPM for qualified views — for a full breakdown of [how much TikTok pays per view](/guides/how-much-does-tiktok-pay-per-view), see our dedicated guide. Instagram pays via ad revenue share and bonus programs (availability varies).
