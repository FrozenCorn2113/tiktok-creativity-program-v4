# TikTok Shop vs Creator Rewards: Which Is Better?

TikTok Shop can drive high commissions, but it’s a different game than Creator Rewards. This guide compares payouts, effort, and who should prioritize each.

<CalloutBox type="info" title="Quick facts">
  <ul>
    <li>TikTok Shop earnings depend on product conversions.</li>
    <li>Creator Rewards pays RPM for qualified views.</li>
    <li>Most creators do best by combining both.</li>
  </ul>
</CalloutBox>

## TikTok Shop overview

You earn by promoting products and generating sales. This is best for creators who can demo or review items.

## Creator Rewards overview

You earn RPM per qualified 1+ minute views. This is best for creators who can build retention.
