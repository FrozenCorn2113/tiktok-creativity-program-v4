# Repurposing TikTok Content for Maximum ROI

Repurposing extends the life of every video. This guide shows how to reuse content without hurting performance.

<CalloutBox type="info" title="Quick facts">
  <ul>
    <li>Post to TikTok first, then repurpose.</li>
    <li>Edit for each platform’s format.</li>
    <li>Reuse your top-performing hooks.</li>
  </ul>
</CalloutBox>

## Repurposing workflow

1. Post TikTok first  
2. Export, remove watermark  
3. Adapt captions for Reels/Shorts  
4. Track performance differences
