# TikTok LIVE Gifts vs Creator Rewards: Which Earns More?

LIVE gifts and Creator Rewards are two different income paths. One rewards real-time engagement, the other rewards qualified views. This guide helps you decide which to prioritize.

<CalloutBox type="info" title="Quick facts">
  <ul>
    <li>LIVE gifts are volatile but can outperform RPM for highly engaged audiences.</li>
    <li>Creator Rewards is more stable when you post consistently.</li>
    <li>Most creators do best by combining both.</li>
  </ul>
</CalloutBox>

## How LIVE gifts work

LIVE gifts depend on:

- Consistent schedule
- Active chat participation
- Strong call-to-action

It’s highly dependent on your **community depth**, not just follower count. TikTok has also introduced [Scaled LIVE Rewards](/guides/tiktok-scaled-live-rewards), a newer program that adjusts payouts based on audience size and engagement metrics during streams.

## How Creator Rewards work

Creator Rewards depends on qualified views on 1+ minute content. It scales with volume and retention. Use the [earnings calculator](/calculators/earnings-calculator) to estimate what your qualified views would earn through Creator Rewards.

## Which earns more?

It depends on your strengths:

- If you thrive live, gifts can be strong.  
- If you’re consistent with edited content, RPM is more stable.
