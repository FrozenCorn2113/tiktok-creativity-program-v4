# TikTok Creator Rewards Australia Eligibility Guide (2026)

If you’re in Australia, the Creator Rewards Program is **not** available. That’s the short answer. The longer answer: you can still build strong income on TikTok, and there are smarter ways to monetize than relying on RPM payouts.

<CalloutBox type="info" title="Quick facts">
  <ul>
    <li>Australia is not eligible for Creator Rewards as of 2026.</li>
    <li>Eligible countries: US, UK, Germany, France, Japan, South Korea, Mexico, Brazil.</li>
    <li>You can still monetize through affiliates, products, services, and brand deals.</li>
  </ul>
</CalloutBox>

## Why Australia is excluded

TikTok’s Creator Rewards Program launched in a small set of countries. Australia hasn’t been added yet. That means there is **no official application path** for Australian accounts, even if you meet the follower and view requirements.

This is frustrating, especially because Australian creators often hit the 1-minute content standards and engagement benchmarks. But the eligibility list is country-based and controlled by TikTok.

## Can you “switch” countries?

Some creators try to use a VPN or a new SIM to appear in an eligible country. This is risky:

- It can trigger account reviews.
- It can mess with ad or audience targeting.
- It can lead to shadowbans or delayed approval.

<CalloutBox type="warning">
  Avoid region spoofing. It’s not sustainable and can damage your account.
</CalloutBox>

## The fastest alternatives for Australian creators

### 1. Affiliate marketing (simple and reliable)

Start with one category of tools or products, then build short video recommendations.

Focus on:  
- Creator tools (editing apps, microphones, lighting)  
- Niche-specific products (fitness gear, music tools, art supplies)

### 2. Brand deals (best for engaged audiences)

Australian brands still invest in creator marketing. You can pitch:

- Sponsored videos  
- Product reviews  
- Long-term partnerships  

Build a one-page media kit with your views, engagement rate, and audience demographics. This raises your pricing power fast.

### 3. Digital products

Create a simple, low-priced offer for your audience:

- Templates  
- Checklists  
- Mini-courses  

If you don’t know what to build, start with a free resource (like a tracker) and listen to what people ask for.

### 4. Services or UGC

You can also monetize by offering:

- UGC video packages  
- Editing services  
- Strategy calls  

This is the quickest way to replace RPM income while you build a larger offer.
