# How to Negotiate Brand Deals as a Small Creator

You don’t need a huge following to land brand deals. You need engagement, clarity, and a simple pitch.

<CalloutBox type="info" title="Quick facts">
  <ul>
    <li>Engagement rate matters more than follower count.</li>
    <li>Clear offers and pricing close deals faster.</li>
    <li>Start with small, niche brands.</li>
  </ul>
</CalloutBox>

## Pricing basics

Start with a baseline:

- Average views × $0.01–$0.03  
- Add extras for usage rights  

## Pitch template

Short and specific:

- Who you are  
- What your audience looks like  
- What you propose  
- Expected results
