# Convert TikTok Business Account to Personal (2026 Guide)

Creator Rewards **requires a personal account**. If you’re on a business account, you’ll need to switch before applying. This guide shows the steps, what you’ll lose, and how to protect your content performance.

<CalloutBox type="info" title="Quick facts">
  <ul>
    <li>Business accounts are not eligible for Creator Rewards.</li>
    <li>Switching takes under a minute and is reversible.</li>
    <li>You may lose some music options and analytics features.</li>
  </ul>
</CalloutBox>

## Step-by-step: switch to personal

1. Open TikTok and go to **Profile**.  
2. Tap the menu (≡) → **Settings and privacy**.  
3. Go to **Account** → **Switch to Personal Account**.  
4. Confirm the switch.

That’s it. You can switch back later if needed.

## What changes after the switch

You may notice:

- Fewer commercial music tracks  
- Slightly different analytics options  
- Access to Creator Rewards tools (once eligible)

<CalloutBox type="warning">
  Switching to personal is required for Creator Rewards, but it can remove some business-only music.
</CalloutBox>

## Best practices after switching

- Post 2–3 new 1+ minute videos.  
- Track your qualified view rate for a week.  
- Avoid switching back until after approval.
