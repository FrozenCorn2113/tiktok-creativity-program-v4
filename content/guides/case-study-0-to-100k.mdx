# From 0 to 100K: A Creator's TikTok Journey

This case study outlines the strategy, content cadence, and system that grew a creator from zero to 100K followers.

<CalloutBox type="info" title="Quick facts">
  <ul>
    <li>30-day series focus</li>

    <li>Consistent hooks and follow-ups</li>

## The strategy

- One niche, one format  
- Weekly batch filming  
- Follow-up content based on comments
