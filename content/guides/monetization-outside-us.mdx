# TikTok Monetization Outside the US: Complete Guide

If you’re outside the US, monetization looks different depending on your country. This guide covers eligibility, RPM expectations, and the best alternatives if Creator Rewards is not available.

<CalloutBox type="info" title="Quick facts">
  <ul>
    <li>Eligible countries: US, UK, Germany, France, Japan, South Korea, Mexico, Brazil.</li>
    <li>RPM varies by country and audience mix.</li>
    <li>Alternatives include affiliates, products, services, and brand deals.</li>
  </ul>
</CalloutBox>

## Eligible countries (Creator Rewards)

As of 2026, Creator Rewards is available in:

- United States  
- United Kingdom  
- Germany  
- France  
- Japan  
- South Korea  
- Mexico  
- Brazil  

If you’re in one of these countries, focus on meeting eligibility and posting 1+ minute content.

## If you’re excluded

If your country is excluded, you should prioritize direct income:

1. Affiliate marketing  
2. Digital products  
3. Services or coaching  
4. Brand deals

## Country-based RPM expectations (general)

RPM usually trends higher in the US, UK, and Germany, and lower in regions with smaller ad markets. Your audience mix matters more than where you live.
