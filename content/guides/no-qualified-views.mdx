# Why Am I Not Getting Qualified Views on TikTok?

If you’re eligible but qualified views are still low, it usually comes down to format, timing, or audience mix. This guide helps you diagnose fast.

<CalloutBox type="info" title="Quick facts">
  <ul>
    <li>Only 1+ minute original videos count.</li>
    <li>Only views after program approval count.</li>
    <li>For You Page views are required.</li>
  </ul>
</CalloutBox>

## Common causes

1. Videos are under 60 seconds  
2. Ineligible formats (Duet, Stitch, Photo Mode)  
3. Posting before approval  
4. Audience mix outside eligible countries  
5. Weak retention signals
