## What to Look for When Buying

**Portrait mode first.** Confirm the tripod or mount can orient your phone vertically before buying. Many photography tripods assume horizontal filming.

**Height for your content type.** Dance and full-body content needs 60"+ height. Desk tutorials need something compact. Travel content needs something that packs down.

**Wireless remote.** Any floor-standing tripod without a remote adds friction to your workflow. Budget for the remote.

**MagSafe vs. clamp.** If you use iPhone 12+, MagSafe is faster. For everything else, a good clamp that holds your phone size is what matters.

## FAQ

**Do I need a tripod for TikTok?**

If you film handheld and the shake bothers you, yes. If you film solo and need to be in the shot, yes. If you're already happy with your setup, no.

**What's the best tripod for TikTok dancing?**

A floor-standing tripod at 60"+ height with a wireless remote. The Sensyne 62" or 72" are the budget calls.

**Can I use a regular camera tripod for TikTok?**

Only if it has a phone holder attachment and can rotate to portrait orientation. Most standard camera tripods assume horizontal. You can buy a phone adapter for a 1/4" screw mount, but check compatibility first.

**What tripod works for TikTok LIVE?**

The ULANZI MA26 desk stand is the best option for fixed-desk LIVE setups, especially if you want to attach a light or mic to the same stand.
