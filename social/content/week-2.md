**Topic:** What "qualified views" actually means on TikTok

- **Tweet 1:** "Qualified views" and "views" are not the same thing on TikTok. Here's why that matters for your paycheck.
- **Tweet 2:** A qualified view is one where the viewer watches for a meaningful duration. Not every view counts toward your Creator Rewards payout.

- **Tweet 4:** To increase qualified views: improve retention in the first 3-5 seconds, target higher-RPM geographies, and keep videos above 60 seconds.

## Thursday (Day 11)

### Facebook -- Text Post (CT2: Quick Tip, expanded)
**Topic:** How to check where your TikTok audience is located (and why it matters for earnings)

**Post copy:**
Your TikTok earnings depend more on WHERE your viewers are than how many views you get. Here's how to check.

1. Open TikTok and go to your profile.
2. Tap the Analytics button (or go to Settings > Creator tools > Analytics).
3. Go to the Followers tab.
4. Scroll to "Top territories."

If most of your viewers are in the US, UK, Canada, or Australia, your RPM will be higher because advertisers pay more to reach those audiences.

If your audience is mostly outside those regions, your RPM will be lower even if your view count is strong. That's not a flaw in the system. Advertiser rates vary by country, and your payout reflects that.

Knowing this helps you make better decisions about when to post (time zone targeting) and what content to create (topics that attract higher-RPM audiences).
