## Thursday (Day 4)

### Facebook -- Text Post (CT2: Quick Tip, expanded)
**Topic:** How to switch from Creator Fund to Creator Rewards (step by step)

**Post copy:**
If you're still on TikTok's old Creator Fund, here's how to switch to Creator Rewards. It takes about 2 minutes.

1. Open TikTok and go to your profile.
2. Tap the menu (three lines, top right) and go to Settings and Privacy.
3. Tap Creator tools.
4. Look for Creator Rewards Program. If you see it, tap to apply.
5. If you don't see it, you either don't meet the requirements yet (10K followers, 100K views in 30 days) or your region isn't eligible.

The switch isn't automatic. You have to opt in. And the pay difference is significant: community-reported RPMs for Creator Rewards run 10-20x higher than the old Creator Fund rates.
