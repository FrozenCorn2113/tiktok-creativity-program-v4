## Phase 2 Growth Deliverables

### Lead Magnet: TikTok Earnings Tracker

Purpose: Convert readers into email subscribers with a useful tracking sheet.  
Target Keywords: TikTok earnings tracker, Creator Rewards tracker.  
User Intent: Get a spreadsheet to track payouts and RPM.  
Success Metrics: Download rate, email signups, weekly retention.  
Next Steps: Add tracker link to the earnings calculator and resource pages.

Recommended sheet structure:
- Date
- Video title
- Views
- Qualified views
- RPM
- Estimated earnings
- Content type
- Notes

Include:
- Weekly summary tab
- Monthly summary tab
- Instructions tab
