## Positioning
TikTok Creator Program Guide is an independent, plain-language resource that helps creators understand eligibility, apply with confidence, and make realistic plans for earnings without hype, spam, or confusing claims.

## Target user + top jobs-to-be-done
- New or aspiring creators who want to know if they qualify.
- Approved creators who need clarity on earnings and payouts.
- Creators who want practical tools and workflows to improve output.

Top jobs:
1. Determine if I qualify and what I’m missing.
2. Apply without mistakes and get approved faster.
3. Understand earnings drivers and plan content accordingly.

## Primary funnel
Traffic -> Start Here -> Email capture (checklist + updates) -> Tools/Affiliate -> Future digital products

## Content pillars
- Eligibility (requirements, content rules, edge cases)
- Apply (walkthrough, review times, troubleshooting)
- Earnings (qualified views, RPM, payouts)
- Tools (planning, editing, analytics, workflow)

## Monetization rules
- Affiliate CTAs appear in: Tools page, inline “recommended tools” blocks inside guides, and the footer disclosure.
- Affiliate CTAs do NOT appear in: Start Here hero, eligibility checklist, FAQ answers, or calculator outputs.
- Any affiliate link is labeled as “Affiliate” or “Curated” and never blocks core guidance.
